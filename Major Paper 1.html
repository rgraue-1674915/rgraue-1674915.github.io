Sea Bear Attacks Rising: New Theory as to Why

By Ryan Graue

In the realm of animal attacks on people they are statistically falling, except for one animal; the sea bear. The attacks of sea bears have started to increase, and it is puzzling the academic and scientific world. Some say it is because of global climate change, forcing them out of their natural habitats in search of new land, while others claim that people are expanding their cities outward and encroaching on the sea bear’s natural habitat. These claims have been debated within the scientific world for quite some time, however, a new one is starting to pick up traction.

This new theory has been making quite a ripple among the scientific community, simply because it suggests scientific and educational community are creating the problem. Their theory revolves around the idea the people are not being taught about sea bears in a sufficient manner. Rather, people are becoming ignorant to sea bears and sea bear attacks. Some individuals have even gone as far to believe that sea bears do not even exist, which for a specific few of them has led to their demise.

The newest sea bear attack, which has put this issue and the new ideas behind it in the spotlight, was on of the best documented animal attacks that the world had ever seen. A tragic event of course, but nonetheless, it has granted the scientific community exactly what they need to answer the question as to why sea bears are attacking.

This most recent attack was captured all on video, in amazing quality during a regular camping trip. The man seeing being attacked, Edward T; was seen being attacked on multiple occasions all because taunting the sea bear and not following proper technique to ward off attacks. His blatant taunting of the sea bear and joking about its existence just minutes before he was attacked is what not only attracted the sea bear, but also made the problem between the two more personal, which only made the sea bear more violent. While on the contrary, the two other members of the group, Robert S and Patrick S; displayed excellent protocol for when sea bear attacks. By drawing the correct sea bear circle, and then also trying to stop Edward from provoking a sea bear.

This video has clearly showed why sea bears attack, but it also helps to justify why the attacks have been escalating. Ever since the early 2000’s there has been a spike in sea bear attacks. This coincidentally was at the same time as legislation was passed were schools do not require to teach about sea bears. This legislation combined with the budget cuts from the housing crisis, made sea bear education the clear target to get dropped from the curriculum.

It does not seem coincidental that sea bear attacks rise at the same time that they stop teaching the subject at school. Plus, with the addition of the new video, only confirms that people have become ignorant to sea bears. Simply by putting two and two together, you should be able to see why this new claim is picking up speed and is also able to be the only explainable reason for why sea bear attacks are rising. Now hopefully ig you ever come face to face with a sea bear, just do not be ignorant.
